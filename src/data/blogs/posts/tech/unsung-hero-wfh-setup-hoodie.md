## 🏗️ The Psychology of the "WFH Uniform"
Why do we care about a hoodie when we aren't even leaving the house? Because of **Enclothed Cognition.** This is the idea that what you wear affects how you think. 
If you stay in your wrinkled pjs all day, your brain stays in "Sleep Mode." But if you put on a high-quality, comfortable hoodie, you flip a switch. You’re cozy, but you’re *ready.* In the Pakistani context, where the line between "Home" and "Office" is blurred by unexpected guests or family demands, having a "Uniform" that looks good for a last-minute Zoom call is a survival strategy.
