### Is it really "Free" to watch?
Technically, some content is free (with ads). For premium stuff (like live sports or latest movies), you need a subscription. But compared to Netflix (Rs. 1,500+) or Disney+ (not even available), a Rs. 10/day or Rs. 100/week plan is extremely affordable for students.

### Why does the app keep crashing?
Local apps still struggle with "Scale." During a Pakistan vs. India match, when 10 million people hit the server at once, it *will* crash. **Pro Tip:** Open the app 15 minutes before the match starts to lock in your session.

### Can I watch these on my Laptop/TV?
Yes! Both Tamasha and Tapmad have web versions and Android TV apps. If your hostel has a "Common Room" TV, installing these apps is the best way to become the "Hero" of the dorm.

### How do I stop the "Push Notifications"?
They are aggressive! Go to your phone settings -> Apps -> Tamasha -> Notifications -> Turn off. Only turn them back on during the PSL season!
