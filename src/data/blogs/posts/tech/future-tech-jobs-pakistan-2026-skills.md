## 🛠️ The "T-Shaped" Skill Stack
Modern employers in Pakistan (and globally) are moving away from hiring "Deep Specialists" who only know one thing. They want people with a **T-Shaped Profile**:
*   **The Horizontal Bar (Broad Knowledge):** You should know a bit about UI/UX, a bit about Cloud Deployment, and a bit about Business Communication.
*   **The Vertical Bar (Deep Expertise):** You pick ONE thing—be it React, Python Data Science, or AWS Infrastructure—and you master it.

---

## 📚 Local Training Paths: PIAIC vs. Saylani vs. Self-Study
In Pakistan, you don't need a four-year degree to start earning, though it helps.
1.  **PIAIC (Presidential Initiative):** Excellent for Cloud-Native and AI foundations. The community is massive, and it gives you a structured syllabus.
2.  **Saylani Welfare:** The "Grassroots" hero. They have trained thousands of developers in React and Mobile Dev for free or at very low cost. Great for those starting from zero.
3.  **The Self-Study Stack:** YouTube + Scrimba + FreeCodeCamp. If you have the discipline, this is the fastest way to stay updated with 2026 trends.

## 👨‍💻 Data & AI: The Entry Point
If you are a student, your goal should be to become "AI-Literate." 
*   **Don't just use AI; build with it.** Learn how to use APIs from Google (AI Studio) and OpenAI. 
*   **Prompt Engineering is a Real Skill.** Knowing how to talk to a machine to get the exact JSON output you need for an app is a job in itself in 2026.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Will AI kill Junior Dev jobs in Pakistan?
**No, but it will raise the bar.** You can't just know "HTML/CSS" anymore. You need to know how to use AI to build 10x faster. The "Junior" of 2026 will be expected to do the work of a "Senior" of 2020.

### Is it worth learning WordPress in 2026?
**Yes, for the local market.** Many small businesses in Pakistan still need WordPress sites. However, to earn more, learn how to build **Headless WordPress** using Next.js.

### What is the best city for tech jobs?
**Lahore** remains the cultural tech hub with the most startups. **Karachi** has the big financial tech houses. **Islamabad** is perfect for remote-first companies and specialized R&D.

### Should I prioritize a degree or a certification?
**Get the degree (for visa/legal reasons) but build the skills (for the job).** A degree opens the door; a GitHub portfolio gets you the seat.
