## 🛠️ The "PTA Politics" Guide: What Should You Buy?

In Pakistan, the "PTA Tax" is often more than the phone's original price. Here is how to navigate it:

1.  **Official PTA Approved:** The safest bet. You get a local warranty (Airlink, Inovi, etc.). It’s more expensive but zero headache.
2.  **CPID Approved:** These are "Software Approved" phones. They work with all SIMs and look official, but you can't "Hard Reset" them or update the software easily. Only buy if you're a tech nerd.
3.  **Non-PTA:** Only buy this as a secondary device for gaming or Wi-Fi use. Don't rely on it for your primary SIM unless you're planning to pay the tax later.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Is 4GB RAM enough in 2025?
Barely. For a smooth experience, look for **"8GB+8GB Extended"** RAM. Android 15 needs more memory to keep apps like WhatsApp and Instagram running in the background.

### Can I trust used phones from OLX?
Only if you can do a "Face-to-Face" meeting. Never send an advance payment. Check the "Battery Health" and "Screen Burn" (common in old Samsungs) before paying.

### Which brand has the best resale value?
In Pakistan, **Samsung** and **iPhone** hold their value the best. If you plan to sell your phone after a year, go with Samsung.

### Do budget phones support fast charging?
Most now support 18W or 33W. Avoid anything that says "Standard 10W Charging"—it will take 4 hours to charge your phone.
