### Is there a catch to the "Free Tier"?
The free tier has "Rate Limits" (e.g., 15 requests per minute). For an individual creator, this is **Plenty**. If you start getting millions of hits, Google expects you to move to the paid Vertex AI platform.

### Do I need to be a programmer?
**No.** If you can write a clear email, you can use AI Studio. The "System Instruction" field is where the magic happens—just speak to it like a smart intern.

### Can it speak Urdu well?
Google AI Studio's Urdu capabilities are currently top-tier. It understands Roman Urdu (*Aap kaise hain?*) and Urdu Script (*آپ کیسے ہیں؟*) with equal ease.
