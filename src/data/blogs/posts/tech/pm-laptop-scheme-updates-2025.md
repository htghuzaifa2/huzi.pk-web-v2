## 🔍 The "Selection Logic" Explained
Many students with 3.5 CGPAs are upset because they weren't selected, while someone with 2.9 was. Why?
1.  **Quota System:** Laptops are divided by university and department. If your department only has 50 laptops and you're the 51st person in your batch, you might miss out even with a high GPA.
2.  **Date of Enrollment:** You must have been a "Regular" student during the eligibility window. If you were a "Private" or "External" student, the system will auto-reject you.
3.  **The "Main Campus" Rule:** This is a hard pill to swallow, but affiliated colleges are often excluded in the initial phases to focus on the university's main campuses.

## 🛠️ The "Laptop-Care" Protocol
These laptops aren't just gifts; they are tools for your future. Treat them like gold.
*   **The Battery Mystery:** Most of these laptops come with decent but mid-range batteries. Don't leave it plugged in 24/7. Use it until it hits 20%, then charge it to 80%. This "Hostel-Hack" will keep your battery healthy for 3 years instead of 1.
*   **The OS Guard:** You get a legitimate copy of Windows 11. **Do not install pirated software.** Use the free Office 365 provided by the HEC. Pirated software is the #1 reason these laptops get "Slow" within 6 months due to background malware.
*   **The Screen Shield:** Invest Rs. 500 in a good screen protector and a padded sleeve. Hostel life is rough; a single drop can end your freelancing career before it starts.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### I lost my "Activation Key," what do I do?
Don't panic. Go to your university’s IT department. Every laptop is registered under your CNIC, and they can help you recover your software licenses.

### Can I sell my laptop?
**Technically, no.** These laptops are marked "Government Property" on the bios level. If you're caught selling it, you could be blacklisted from all future government scholarship and laptop schemes. Plus, why would you sell a tool that can make you 10x its value in 6 months?

### What if my laptop breaks?
There is usually a **1-year warranty** provided by the contractor (like Dell, HP, or Lenovo). Check the sticker on the bottom of the laptop for the service center address.

### My CGPA is exactly 2.0. Can I still apply?
The 2025 merit list generally starts at 2.8 and goes up. If you're below that, focus on your studies and aim for the next phase in 2-3 years.
