## 🔵 The Rosenior Revolution: A Gamble?
Chelsea's decision to hire **Liam Rosenior** was a shock.
A young, progressive manager with a "Championship" background taking over the most chaotic club in the world?
It sounds like a Netflix drama.
*   **The Style:** Rosenior hates "long balls." He wants playing out from the back, even if it gives fans heart attacks.
*   **The Logic:** Todd Boehly (Chelsea owner) wants a "Project Manager." Someone who can develop the 500 wonderkids they bought.
*   **The Risk:** Arsenal is the worst possible opponent for this. If you make a mistake playing out from the back, **Declan Rice** will punish you.
