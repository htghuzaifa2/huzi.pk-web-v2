## 🇵🇰 4. Why Pakistanis Should Watch
American Football is actually very similar to Cricket (Test matches), not Soccer.
1.  **It's Turn-Based:** One team attacks (bats), one defends (bowls).
2.  **Strategy:** Each play is like a delivery. The captain (QB) changes the field. The bowler (Linebacker) changes the grip.
3.  **Commercials:** Okay, there are too many ads. But that gives you time to make chai.
