## 🟢 1. The Setup: Aristocracy vs The Workers
Real Betis play football like they are attending a gala dinner. Elegant, stylish, expensive (Isco, Fekir, Lo Celso).
Elche ply their trade in the Segunda Division (Second Tier). They are the workers. They run, they fight, they tackle.
*   **The Huzi Analogy:** This was **Lahore Qalandars (Betis)** vs **Peshawar Zalmi (Elche)**. One has the glamour; the other has the grit.
